Model:We selected the model as our general biological engineering special prize because it facilitated the design of a device capable of detecting trimethylamine (TMA) through breath analysis. This model incorporates verified data, equations, and assumptions from existing literature, significantly enhancing its validity. Furthermore, it has provided the entire team with a deeper understanding of the challenges associated with detecting trimethylamine by demonstrating a clear change and a positive correlation between the current density at a steady voltage potential and increasing concentrations of TMA.
